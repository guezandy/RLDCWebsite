Why come to RLDC?

Unite

Strengthen the bonds amongst individual SHPE chapters, as well as, reinforcing a strong working relationship between all Region 7 chapters. Network with the people who may land you your next job or become your business partner.

Grow

Develop as a young professional in the STEM world. Attend workshops that will help you build your “brand.” Gain a thorough understanding of Transformational Leadership, and how through it, one can develop strong and lasting SHPE Familias. Give yourself an advantage when starting your career.

Miami

Experience Warmth. Immerse in Culture. Witness Beauty.​
